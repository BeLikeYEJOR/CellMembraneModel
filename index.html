CELL MEMBRANE MODEL

By: Yegor Lantsman

Nucleas

The Nucleus of a cell membrane is like a control center of all The Children Cells in the cell Membrane. It is important because the Nucleus makes the other cells do their job.

Mitochondria

The Mitochondria of a cell membrane is a Powerhouse of all the other cells. It is important because the Mitochondria lets other cells work.

Chloroplast

The Chloroplast of a cell membrane is like a storage center. It holds all the sweets and the greens. It is important because the Chloroplast gives energy and power to all the other Cells.

Vacuole

The Vacuole of a Cell Membrane is like a water holder. It Holds all the waters and liquids that are exclusive for the Cell. It is important becuase the Vacuole gives the other cells nessesary liquids that help them work.

Cytosol

The Cytosol of a Cell Membrane is like the ground of the cell. It lets the other cells move, work, and stand still.It is important because the Cytosol lets the cell do their work and lets them move around

Cell Wall

The Cell Wall of a Cell Membrane is like the walls of the cell. It is importamt because it makes it so the cell doesent mess it self up and keeps all the other cells in place .
